# Business Model Canvas: promptforge

## Value Propositions
- A specialized platform for comprehensive testing and evaluation of Large Language Models focused on specific instructions and prompt edge cases
- Streamlines AI development workflows by identifying prompt vulnerabilities and model weaknesses before deployment
- Provides structured testing methodologies that reduce development time by 40% and improve model robustness
- Enables systematic prompt engineering with automated edge case discovery and performance benchmarking
- Delivers actionable insights for improving model alignment and reducing hallucination rates

## Customer Segments
- **AI/ML Development Teams**: Tech companies building custom LLM applications requiring rigorous prompt testing
- **Prompt Engineering Specialists**: Professionals focused on optimizing and securing prompt interactions
- **AI Research Institutions**: Academia and research labs needing robust evaluation frameworks for their models
- **Enterprise AI Deployers**: Organizations implementing LLMs in production requiring reliability assurance
- **AI Safety and Alignment Teams**: Specialists focused on model behavior and alignment with human values
- **AI Startups**: Early-stage companies needing cost-effective testing infrastructure without building in-house solutions

## Channels
- **Direct Digital Platform**: Web-based interface accessible through Axentx's main portal
- **API Integration**: RESTful API for seamless integration into existing AI development workflows
- **Cloud Marketplace**: Distribution through major cloud provider marketplaces (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- **Open Source Community**: Community version with freemium model driving adoption
- **Academic Partnerships**: Research programs and university collaborations
- **Developer Conferences**: Industry events and hackathons targeting AI/ML professionals

## Revenue Streams
- **Subscription Tiers**: 
  - Basic: $99/month (up to 1,000 tests/month)
  - Professional: $299/month (up to 10,000 tests/month)
  - Enterprise: Custom pricing (unlimited tests, advanced features)
- **API Usage**: Pay-per-test model for API-based testing ($0.10 per test)
- **Enterprise SLAs**: Premium support with guaranteed response times and custom SLAs
- **Custom Testing Suites**: Bespoke testing frameworks for specific industry needs
- **Data Insights**: Premium benchmark reports and trend analysis
- **Integration Partnerships**: Revenue sharing with AI platform providers
