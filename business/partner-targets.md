# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### 1. **LangChain**
- **Rationale**: LangChain is a popular framework for developing applications powered by language models. Integrating with LangChain will allow users to seamlessly incorporate PromptForge into their existing LangChain workflows.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: LangChain is open-source, so there are no direct limits, but cloud services may have usage limits.
- **Integration Effort**: S
- **Value-Add**: Solves the job of streamlining the testing and evaluation of LLMs within existing LangChain projects.

### 2. **Hugging Face**
- **Rationale**: Hugging Face is a leading platform for training and deploying machine learning models. Integrating with Hugging Face will provide users with access to a vast library of pre-trained models.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Hugging Face offers a free tier with limited API calls.
- **Integration Effort**: M
- **Value-Add**: Solves the job of accessing and evaluating a wide range of pre-trained models for specific instructions and prompt edge cases.

### 3. **AWS Bedrock**
- **Rationale**: AWS Bedrock provides a managed service for building and scaling generative AI applications. Integrating with AWS Bedrock will allow users to leverage AWS's infrastructure for testing and evaluating LLMs.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: AWS offers a free tier with limited usage.
- **Integration Effort**: L
- **Value-Add**: Solves the job of scaling and managing LLM testing and evaluation within AWS's infrastructure.
