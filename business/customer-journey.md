```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for PromptForge

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developer or researcher encounters difficulties in testing and evaluating LLMs, such as inconsistent results or edge cases.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness of specialized tools for LLM testing, reliance on manual methods, and time-consuming processes.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, and a sense of inefficiency.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blogs, tutorials) and case studies showcasing the benefits of efficient LLM testing.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to the product website or landing page.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Discovery of PromptForge through search engines, social media, or word-of-mouth.
- **Friction Points**: Skepticism about the tool's effectiveness, comparison with existing solutions, and perceived complexity.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity, skepticism, and hope for a better solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Free trials, demo videos, and detailed documentation highlighting ease of use and effectiveness.
- **Metric**: Number of sign-ups for free trials or demo requests.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Signing up for a free trial or using a demo version of PromptForge.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity, learning curve, and integration with existing workflows.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement, cautious optimism, and potential frustration if setup is not smooth.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Seamless onboarding process, responsive customer support, and quick setup guides.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial users to paid subscribers.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Positive experience during the trial period, leading to a decision to purchase.
- **Friction Points**: Pricing concerns, perceived value, and long-term commitment.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, confidence, and a sense of relief.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Competitive pricing plans, flexible subscription options, and personalized onboarding.
- **Metric**: Number of paid subscriptions and customer lifetime value (CLV).

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Successful implementation and positive ROI from using PromptForge.
- **Friction Points**: Scaling usage across teams, additional feature needs, and integration with other tools.
- **User Emotions**: Loyalty, advocacy, and a desire for continuous improvement.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Advanced features, enterprise solutions, and regular updates based on user feedback.
- **Metric**: Number of upsells, cross-sells, and referrals.
```
